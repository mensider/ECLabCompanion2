GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

DO’S

Before starting the lab work ensure all instruments are working in good condition.

  • Switch on the CRO and check for the traces for both channels.
  • Check all meters for errors and bend needles.
  • Check all diodes and transistors before connecting in the circuit.
  • Check all op-amps by setting up simple zero crossing detector circuit.
  • Be soft while inserting and removing ICs from the bread board.
  • While using digital ICs make sure that the supply voltage is exactly 5V.
  • Make sure that power is applied before applying input o any electronic circuit (where ever power supply is required).
  • Check the conditions of the circuits after setting up the circuit.
  • Switch off all Equipments and arrange your seats properly leaving the lab.

DON’TS

  • Do not switch off the CRO often.
  • Do not leave bits of wire in the bread board.
  • Do not short circuit the output terminals of the power supply.
  • Do not touch on the back side of the equipment unnecessarily.
  • While working with line supply do not touch the bread board or any other devices with the supply on.
